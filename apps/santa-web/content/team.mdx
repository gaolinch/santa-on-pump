# Team

## Core Team

The Santa project is built by a dedicated team of blockchain enthusiasts and developers committed to transparency and community engagement.

### Founders

The founding team manages the initial phase with open reporting and transparency. All treasury operations are secured by a multi-signature wallet for enhanced security.

### Multisig

All treasury operations are secured by a multi-signature wallet (3-of-5) to ensure no single point of failure.

### Transparency

- All treasury addresses are public
- All distributions are verifiable on-chain
- Daily proofs published for each gift
- End-of-season impact report
